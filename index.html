Nigeria's Voice, Digitally Amplified

9jaPolls is a digital polling platform designed specifically for Nigerians to express their opinions on local and national issues. By leveraging mobile and web technologies, we aim to increase civic engagement and bridge the gap between citizens and policymakers.

Our Goals:

  • Increase civic engagement among Nigerians
  • Provide real-time data on public opinion to policymakers
  • Foster transparency in governance through data-driven insights
  • Empower citizens to have a say in matters affecting their communities

Key Features

Accessibility

  • Web Application with responsive design
  • Mobile Apps for Android and iOS
  • SMS Integration for feature phones

Advanced Technology

  • AI and Machine Learning for data analysis
  • Blockchain for transparent results
  • Geolocation-based polling

User-Friendly

  • Multi-language support
  • Real-time results visualization
  • Social media integration

Security & Transparency

  • User authentication and verification
  • End-to-end encryption
  • Open API for researchers

Impact and Benefits

For Citizens

  • Easy access to voice opinions
  • Increased awareness of public sentiment
  • Platform for community-level decision making

For Government

  • Real-time feedback on policies
  • Data-driven decision-making tools
  • Improved citizen engagement and trust

For NGOs and Researchers

  • Valuable data for social research
  • Platform for conducting targeted surveys

Addressing Nigerian-Specific Challenges

Digital Divide

SMS integration and USSD capabilities to ensure accessibility for all.

Diverse Demographics

Multi-language support and intuitive UI to cater to all Nigerians.

Data Costs

Partnerships with telcos for zero-rating of the platform.

Credibility Concerns

Robust verification system and transparent methodology.
